Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar, located in southeastern Bangladesh, is a captivating coastal town renowned for possessing the world's longest natural sea
beach. Its undering stretch of golden sands, stretching approximately 120 kilometers along the Bay of Bengal,attarcts visitors from all over
the globe. Apart from its picturesque beaches. Cox's Bazar is also blessed with lush hills, serene Buddhist temples, and vibrant local markets.
The town's welcoming atmosphere and warm hospitality of its locals make it a popular destination for both local tourists and international
travelers seeking a tranquil and rejuvenating retreat. Additionally, Cox's Bazar offers opportunities for water sports, beachside relaxation,
and breathtaking sunsets, making it a memorable destination that captivates the hearts of all who visit.
